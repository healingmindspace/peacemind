# Platform Review — Strengths & Risks

## What Works

**The Trust Stack is the real moat.** Anyone can build a mood tracker. The combination of personal data + AI patterns + expert validation is hard to replicate. YouTube influencers can't track your PHQ-9 score over time, and generic apps don't have licensed professionals reviewing the guidance.

**"How you feel" as the metric is powerful.** Move tracking mood shift instead of calories, Heal tracking patterns instead of just logging — this reframes wellness from performance to awareness. That's a meaningful philosophical difference.

**The cross-app data story is compelling.** "On days you exercised, your anxiety score was 40% lower" — that insight only exists when Heal + Move + Health share data. No single app can do that.

**The gentle tone is a real differentiator.** Most health apps feel clinical or pushy. "Even 5 minutes counts" and "getting out of bed is real progress" — that's the kind of voice people actually come back to.

## Risk to Watch

**The forum is the riskiest piece.** Moderation at scale is hard, and one bad experience in a mental health community can do real harm. Recommendation: launch small (invite-only, expert-moderated) before opening it up.

## Bottom Line

A platform you'd recommend to someone who asked "where do I start with my mental health?"
